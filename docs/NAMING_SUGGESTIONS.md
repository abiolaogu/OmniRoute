# OmniRoute - Naming Suggestions

## Recommended Names with Domain Availability

Based on the platform's focus on **commerce, logistics, gig economy, and emerging markets**, here are 5 naming suggestions with high likelihood of domain availability:

---

## 1. **Distrolytics**

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Meaning** | Distribution + Analytics |
| **Domain** | distrolytics.com, distrolytics.io |
| **Strengths** | Unique, memorable, implies data-driven distribution |
| **Target** | B2B distributors, FMCG companies |
| **Tagline** | "The Intelligence Behind Distribution" |

## Naming Criteria Used

1. **Memorability**: Easy to spell and remember
2. **Uniqueness**: Not easily confused with competitors
3. **Domain availability**: Likely to have available .com or .io
4. **Global appeal**: Works across languages/cultures
5. **Scalability**: Name can grow with the company
6. **SEO-friendly**: Contains relevant keywords
