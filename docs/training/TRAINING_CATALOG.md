## Overview

OmniRoute provides a comprehensive training program designed to ensure all platform participants can effectively use the platform. Training is available in multiple formats to accommodate different learning styles and schedules.

## Certification Programs

### OmniRoute Certified User (OCU)
- **Duration**: 4 hours (self-paced)
- **Prerequisites**: None
- **Exam**: 50 questions, 75% passing score
- **Validity**: 2 years
- **Topics**: Platform navigation, basic operations, mobile app

### OmniRoute Certified Administrator (OCA)
- **Duration**: 8 hours (self-paced or instructor-led)
- **Prerequisites**: OCU certification
- **Exam**: 80 questions, 80% passing score
- **Validity**: 2 years
- **Topics**: User management, configuration, security, troubleshooting

### OmniRoute Integration Specialist (OIS)
- **Duration**: 12 hours (instructor-led + labs)
- **Prerequisites**: OCA certification, development experience
- **Exam**: Practical + written (85% passing)
- **Validity**: 2 years
- **Topics**: API integration, webhooks, SDK usage, custom development

### OmniRoute Financial Operations (OFO)
- **Duration**: 6 hours (instructor-led)
- **Prerequisites**: OCU certification
- **Exam**: 60 questions, 80% passing score
- **Validity**: 2 years
- **Topics**: Payment processing, credit, ATC, reconciliation
