## Positioning Strategy

### Primary Positioning Statement

> **For FMCG manufacturers and distributors** who need to modernize their route-to-market, **OmniRoute is the unified commerce platform** that combines B2B/B2C e-commerce, distribution management, embedded finance, and gig workforce **in a single system**. Unlike point solutions like Shopify (no DMS), FieldAssist (no commerce), or TradeDepot (marketplace model), **OmniRoute gives you complete control** of your customer relationships while providing all the tools to scale efficiently.

## Conclusion

OmniRoute's competitive position is defined by **breadth** (unified platform), **depth** (emerging market expertise), and **differentiation** (gig economy integration).

No competitor offers the complete package:
- TradeDepot = Commerce without DMS or gig economy
- FieldAssist = DMS without commerce or finance
- Shopify = Commerce without distribution or emerging market depth
- Sabi = Infrastructure without full solution

**We win by being the ONLY platform that does it all.**
