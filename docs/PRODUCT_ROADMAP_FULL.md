# OmniRoute Commerce Platform
# Product Roadmap 2025-2027

---

## Executive Summary

OmniRoute is building the **Commerce Operating System for Emerging Markets** - a unified platform that eliminates friction across the entire manufacturer-to-consumer value chain while creating economic opportunities for millions of gig workers.

---

## Vision Statement

> "By 2027, every FMCG manufacturer, distributor, retailer, and logistics provider in Africa will use OmniRoute as their commerce backbone, powering $10B+ in annual GMV while providing livelihoods for 1M+ gig workers."
